Dokutela



Enabling vulnerable chronic patients access to medication during and post the COVID-19 pandemic. It is a USSD based platform that is user friendly and widespread.

Our Services

Drug Connect

Dokutela enables the patient with a pharmacy ID have access to their medicine at a nearby clinic after completion of a questionnaire.

Doctor Connect

Dokutela allows the patient to book appointments with a doctor of choice at a government hospital of choice upon completion of the survey.

Pharmacist Connect

Dokutela allows the patient to respond to a doctor-approved health questionnaire to assess the health progress of the patient from time to time.
